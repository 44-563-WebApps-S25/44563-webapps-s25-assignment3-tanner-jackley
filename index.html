How to Make a PB&J Sandwich

  1. Get Supplies
    • 2 slices of bread
    • Peanut Butter
    • Jelly (perferably strawberry)
  2. Put peanut butter on both slices of bread
  3. Put jelly on one slice of bread
  4. Put slices together and enjoy!
